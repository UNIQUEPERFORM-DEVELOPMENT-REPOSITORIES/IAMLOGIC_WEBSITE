FAQ

Pricing questions, answered straight

Why don't you publish the per-user rates?

Because the honest number depends on user count, product mix and deployment scope — a published rate would just be the ceiling. Tell us your user count and we'll return a specific quote, typically within two business days.

Is there a proof of concept before we commit?

Yes — we run scoped POCs against your actual applications and success criteria. It's the fastest way to validate legacy-app coverage and connector fit before any licence commitment.

How does IamLogic pricing compare with global vendors?

Materially lower total cost of ownership — direct engineering support instead of tiered support contracts, no forced cloud migration, and services priced in rupees by a Bengaluru team rather than global consulting rates.

Do Access Manager and IGA have to be bought together?

No. Each product licenses independently. Buying both earns platform pricing and removes any integration cost between them.
