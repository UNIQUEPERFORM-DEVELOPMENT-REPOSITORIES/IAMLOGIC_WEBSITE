The challenges

What we hear from teams like yours

Data sovereignty is non-negotiable

Citizen and employee identity data must remain within India — often within your own data centre — which rules out SaaS-only identity vendors.

Mixed application estates

Departments run decades of applications side by side: new portals, NIC-hosted systems, and legacy client-server software.

Contractor and transfer churn

Frequent transfers, deputations and contractor rotations leave live accounts behind in system after system.

CERT-In directions require 180-day log retention within India and six-hour incident reporting; procurement increasingly favours indigenous software under Make in India. An on-premises IamLogic deployment keeps every credential, log and identity record inside your perimeter.
