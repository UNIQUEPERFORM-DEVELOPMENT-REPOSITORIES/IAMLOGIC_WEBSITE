Use case

Access reviews that produce evidence, not spreadsheets

Auditors don't ask whether you have an access-review policy. They ask for the evidence: who reviewed what, when, what was revoked, and how fast. IamLogic IGA runs certification campaigns that generate that evidence as a by-product of doing the review — no spreadsheet assembly required.

The challenges

What we hear from teams like yours

Spreadsheet certifications

Exports, emails and manual merges consume weeks per cycle and produce evidence auditors distrust.

Rubber-stamp reviews

Reviewers approve everything because they lack context on what an entitlement means.

Revocations that never land

A 'revoke' decision in a spreadsheet doesn't remove access in the target system.
