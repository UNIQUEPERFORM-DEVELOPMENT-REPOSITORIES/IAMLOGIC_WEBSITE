The challenges

What we hear from teams like yours

Rotating clinical staff

Residents, locums and visiting consultants need fast access on arrival and reliable removal on departure.

Shared workstations and shared logins

Ward computers invite credential sharing unless SSO makes individual login effortless.

Regulated manufacturing systems

Pharma quality and manufacturing systems require controlled, auditable access for GxP expectations.

Under the DPDP Act and its 2025 Rules, health data processors must implement reasonable security safeguards including access control and access logging — with penalties up to ₹250 crore for failures. See our DPDP compliance hub for the control mapping.
