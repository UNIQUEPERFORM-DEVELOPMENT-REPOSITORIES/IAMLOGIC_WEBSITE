Hybrid work broke the assumption that employees log in from the office network. IamLogic treats location as a signal, not a boundary: the same user gets frictionless access from a trusted context and stronger verification from an unknown one — with governance watching the whole picture.

Yes — context policies are evaluated per application, so a remote login to email and a remote login to your finance system can require different assurance levels.
