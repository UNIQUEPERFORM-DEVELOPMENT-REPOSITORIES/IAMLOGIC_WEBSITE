Use case

Zero Trust access, built on identity

Zero Trust starts from one premise: no login is trusted because of where it comes from. Every access is verified, every privilege is justified, and verification adapts to risk. IamLogic implements the identity pillar of Zero Trust — the pillar every other control depends on.

Is Zero Trust achievable without ripping out our network?

Yes — Zero Trust is an operating model, not a product purchase. Starting with identity (adaptive authentication + least-privilege governance) delivers most of the risk reduction without touching network architecture.
