The challenges

What we hear from teams like yours

Legacy core systems without modern auth

Core banking and settlement applications often cannot speak SAML or OIDC, leaving them outside your SSO and MFA perimeter.

Audit findings on dormant and excess access

Inspection reports repeatedly flag orphan accounts, shared credentials and access that outlived the employee's role.

Manual access reviews that consume quarters

Spreadsheet-driven certifications across hundreds of applications exhaust teams and still miss conflicts.

The RBI Cybersecurity Framework and Master Directions on IT governance expect banks to enforce least privilege, review access periodically, protect authentication with multi-factor controls, and retain audit trails. IamLogic maps these expectations to concrete product controls — see our RBI compliance hub for the full mapping.

FAQ

Common questions

Can IamLogic run fully on-premises inside our data centre?

Yes. Both Access Manager and IamLogic IGA deploy on-premises, in your private cloud, or hybrid — with high availability and DR topologies. Your identity data never has to leave your environment or India.

How do you handle applications that have no API?

IamLogic IGA integrates provisioning for non-API applications through ticketing workflows (ServiceNow, Jira and others), and Access Manager's browser plugin delivers SSO with credential auto-fill and rotation for legacy interfaces.

Does IamLogic support our existing Active Directory?

Yes — real-time AD synchronisation, role-based access control on top of AD groups, and secure credential routing are built into Access Manager.
