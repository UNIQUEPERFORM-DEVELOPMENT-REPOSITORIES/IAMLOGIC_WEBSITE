Use case

Joiner–Mover–Leaver automation that closes the gaps

Every identity risk story starts with a lifecycle event someone missed: the joiner who waited two weeks for access, the mover who kept both old and new privileges, the leaver whose account outlived them by a year. IamLogic IGA automates all three transitions, driven by your HR system.
