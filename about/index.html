About IamLogic

Identity security should not require a foreign cloud and a foreign invoice

We started IamLogic in 2021 with a simple observation: Indian enterprises were paying global prices for identity products designed around someone else's regulations — or settling for point tools that couldn't survive an enterprise audit. We build the third option.

What we build

Two products, engineered as one

Access Manager handles the front door — SSO, adaptive MFA, passwordless and context-based policy across modern and legacy applications. IamLogic IGA handles the governance — lifecycle automation, access certification, role management and segregation of duties.

Both deploy on-premises, in the cloud or hybrid; both are extensible in Python; both generate the audit evidence that DPDP, RBI, SEBI CSCRF and IRDAI actually ask for. That combination — enterprise depth, deployment freedom, Indian-regulation fluency — is the product thesis.

How we work

Certified, close to the customer

IamLogic operates an ISO/IEC 27001:2022 certified information security management system covering the full product lifecycle — we hold ourselves to the standard we help customers meet.

Support and engineering sit in the same Bengaluru office. When a bank hits a high-severity issue at 2 a.m., the person on the call can read the source code. That proximity — of engineering to support, and of the company to its customers' regulators — is deliberate.
