Products

Two products. One decision: who can access what, and should they?

Most vendors sell you either the front door or the audit trail. IamLogic builds both — designed together, deployed your way.

Access Manager

The front door, everywhere

Single sign-on across modern and legacy applications, adaptive multi-factor authentication, passwordless login and context-based policies — including the browser plugin that brings twenty-year-old apps under modern protection.

  • SSO — SAML · OIDC · OAuth2 · RADIUS
  • MFA — WebAuthn · push · TOTP · SMS/Email OTP · phone
  • Context-based authentication with Python-scriptable policies
  • Legacy app SSO with automatic password rotation

IamLogic IGA

The governance behind it

Who has access, why do they have it, and should they still? Lifecycle automation, access certification, role management and segregation of duties — with the audit evidence generated as you work, not assembled before inspections.

  • Joiner–Mover–Leaver automation from your HR system
  • Access certifications with enforced revocation
  • SoD policies · role mining · just-in-time access
  • Python connector framework + ticketing for non-API apps

Why IamLogic

Global-grade capability. Indian-regulation fluency.

Global suites treat India as a locale. Indian point-tools treat enterprise as a stretch goal. IamLogic was engineered for exactly this intersection.

Built for Indian regulation, out of the box

DPDP Act, RBI, SEBI CSCRF and IRDAI expectations mapped directly to product controls — access logs, certifications, SoD and MFA that produce audit evidence by default.

Your data centre, your rules

Deploy fully on-premises, in your private cloud, or hybrid. Identity data, credentials and logs stay in India — or never leave your perimeter at all.

Adapts to your estate, legacy included

Python-based connectors integrate any application; browser-plugin SSO covers the legacy systems other vendors scope out; ticketing workflows reach apps without APIs.

Two focused products, one identity fabric

Access Manager decides who gets in and how. IamLogic IGA governs what they hold and why. Authentication signals feed governance decisions automatically.

The platform

Better together, by design

Authentication signals from Access Manager — unused logins, risky contexts, dormant sessions — flow into IGA reviews and policies. Governance decisions flow back as access changes. One fabric, no integration project.
